ELIGIBILITY. TO APPEAR AT UNIVERSITY EXAMINATION.

A student shall be deemed to have satisfactory kept terms for first year or any semester provided He/she possesses a testimonial of having:-

  1. Attended at least 90% of the total lectures and 100 % practical and tutorials of every subject of the first year or concerned semester.
  2. Carried out the prescribed laboratory work/teamwork and other curricular assignment satisfactorily.
  3. Shown satisfactory progress in studies as approved by HOD and Principal.
  4. Shown good conduct and character as approved by HOD and principal.
  5. Appeared in all internal (Session) tests conducted by College.
  6. If HE/She had completed the team work satisfactory than and than HE/ She is allowed to seat in Annual/ Semester Examination.
  • Internal Tests are conduct to judge the progressive Academic Performance of the student. If it is found dissatisfactory HE/ She will be liable to detention in the university Examination as per the University rules. Hence all the students must appear in all internal Tests.

DETENTION :

A student will be detained from appearing in University Exams if

  1. He/She has not submitted the term-work of the concerned. semester/year.
  2. His/her presence in lectures/practicals/tutorials is found 10 be indeaquate
  3. He/She has not shown satisfactory academis progress during the term.
